# 03) Problem and Market

## Market Problems
- Fragmented tooling (POS, inventory, CRM disconnected) causes data silos.
- Manual kitchen and stock coordination introduces errors and delays.
- Campaign and retention initiatives lack transaction-level context.
- Legacy on-prem POS systems fail multi-branch scalability.

## Market Opportunity
- Global restaurant digitization is accelerating.
- Multi-location operators require unified control planes.
- AI-driven optimization demand is rising (forecasting, staffing, menu mix).

## Target Value
- Single operational truth across front-of-house and back-of-house.
- Faster decision cycles via real-time metrics.
- Lower operational leakage (waste, voids, stock mismatch).
- Stronger customer lifetime value through integrated CRM/marketing.
