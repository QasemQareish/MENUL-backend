# 14) Analytics and AI

## KPI Framework
- **Sales:** gross/net sales, AOV, channel mix, discount leakage.
- **Operations:** ticket time, kitchen SLA, void/refund rate.
- **Inventory:** turnover, wastage %, stockout frequency, COGS variance.
- **Customer:** repeat rate, retention cohorts, loyalty redemption, campaign conversion.

## Data Architecture
- Event-driven ingestion from transactional services.
- Curated semantic models for finance, operations, and customer domains.
- Near-real-time dashboards + scheduled executive reporting.

## AI Roadmap
1. Demand forecasting by daypart/weather/events.
2. Smart menu recommendations (upsell/cross-sell).
3. Dynamic prep-time prediction and staffing hints.
4. Campaign optimization using uplift modeling.

## AI Governance
- Model explainability logs for decision support.
- Bias/performance monitoring by region and segment.
- Human override for automated suggestions.
