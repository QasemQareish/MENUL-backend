# 19) Feasibility

## Technical
Feasible with staged evolution: harden current backend, migrate to PostgreSQL, introduce event-driven capabilities, and formalize tenant isolation controls.

## Economic
High long-term upside through ARPU expansion (module upsells, enterprise tiers) but requires upfront investment in reliability, compliance, and global operations.

## Operational
Adoption feasibility is high if onboarding playbooks and role-focused UX are executed; franchise/enterprise rollouts require stronger change-management and SLA support models.
