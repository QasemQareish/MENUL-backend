# 20) SWOT

## Strengths
- Unified operational + commercial platform design.
- Multi-tenant and white-label readiness.
- API-first architecture supporting ecosystem integrations.

## Weaknesses
- Current baseline requires significant production hardening.
- Offline sync and global compliance add architectural complexity.

## Opportunities
- AI-driven operational optimization and marketing uplift.
- Expansion into enterprise franchise and cross-brand groups.

## Threats
- Strong incumbents in POS and hospitality SaaS.
- Regulatory differences across markets increase rollout complexity.
